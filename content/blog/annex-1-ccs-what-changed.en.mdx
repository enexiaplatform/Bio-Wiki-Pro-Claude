# Annex 1 (2022): what actually changed

The 2022 revision of EU GMP Annex 1 is the biggest sterile-manufacturing update
in decades. If you read one thing, read this.

## 1. CCS becomes the organising principle

A documented **Contamination Control Strategy** now ties every control together,
site-wide. It's not a new binder — it's how you demonstrate the whole system is
coherent and risk-justified.

## 2. Quality Risk Management throughout

QRM is woven into every section. Expect to justify decisions (sampling sites,
limits, interventions) against documented risk — not precedent.

## 3. Monitoring expectations rise

Continuous viable and non-viable monitoring in Grade A/B, with trending that
detects drift before limits are breached.

## What to do now

Map your existing controls to a CCS document, identify gaps, and make sure your
monitoring program produces trends — not just pass/fail results.
