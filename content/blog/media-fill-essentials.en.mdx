# Media fills: the test that validates your people

A media fill (aseptic process simulation) replaces product with sterile growth
media and runs the aseptic process end to end. It validates the line, the
environment, **and the operators**.

## Acceptance criteria (Annex 1)

- Up to 5,000 units: **zero** contaminated units (one positive triggers a revalidation/investigation).
- The target is **zero growth** — any positive is a serious signal.

## Worst-case design

Simulate the hardest conditions: maximum interventions, longest run, shift
changes, line-speed extremes — not an easy "best day" run.

## Why one unit matters

A single contaminated media-fill unit can halt production and trigger a
data-rich investigation. That's the point: media fills surface technique and
design weaknesses *before* they reach product.
