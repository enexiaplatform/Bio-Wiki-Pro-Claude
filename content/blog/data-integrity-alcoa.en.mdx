# Data Integrity & the ALCOA+ principles

Data integrity is the precondition for every quality decision. Inspectors now
scrutinise data as closely as they scrutinise product.

## What ALCOA+ covers

- **Attributable** — traceable to who did it and when.
- **Legible** — readable, no obliteration.
- **Contemporaneous** — recorded at the time of the activity.
- **Original** — the original record or a certified true copy.
- **Accurate** — correct and internally consistent.

The "+": **Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available**.

## Why it matters

Most recent FDA warning letters cite data integrity failings (audit trails
disabled, records overwritten). A healthy QMS treats audit-trail review as a
routine, scheduled activity.
