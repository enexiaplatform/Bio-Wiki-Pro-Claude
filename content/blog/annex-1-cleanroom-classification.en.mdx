# Cleanroom classification under Annex 1 (2022)

The 2022 revision of EU GMP Annex 1 tightens requirements for sterile
manufacturing and puts the **Contamination Control Strategy (CCS)** at the
centre.

## Grade A–D

| Grade | Description | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | High-risk operations | Filling point, aseptic connections |
| B | Background for Grade A (aseptic) | Room surrounding the A zone |
| C, D | Less critical clean areas | Compounding, primary packing |

## Notable changes

- **CCS** is mandatory and site-wide.
- Continuous particle & microbial monitoring in Grade A/B.
- Emphasis on quality risk management (QRM) over recipe-following.
