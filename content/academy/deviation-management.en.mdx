# Deviation Management

A deviation is any departure from an approved procedure, specification, or
standard. How quickly and rigorously you handle deviations is a top audit focus.

## Lifecycle

1. **Detect & document** — record what happened, when, and immediate impact.
2. **Classify** — minor / major / critical by potential product impact.
3. **Investigate** — root cause for major/critical.
4. **Action & close** — corrections, CAPA where needed, on-time closure.

## Severity tiers

- **Minor:** no product-quality impact.
- **Major:** potential quality impact, requires investigation.
- **Critical:** may affect safety, efficacy, or compliance — escalate.

## Common findings

Late closures, weak root-cause analysis, and recurring deviations without a CAPA
are the most-cited deviation-system observations.
