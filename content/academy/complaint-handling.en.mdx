# Complaint Handling & Recall

A complaint is feedback that a product may not meet quality, safety, or efficacy
expectations. A controlled complaint system is a regulatory requirement and an
early-warning signal.

## Lifecycle

1. **Receive & log** — capture details, product, batch, reporter.
2. **Assess** — patient risk, possible defect, reportability to authorities.
3. **Investigate** — root cause; correlate with batch records and other complaints.
4. **Respond & close** — corrective action, CAPA, and reply to the complainant.

## When it escalates to recall

If investigation indicates a defect that could affect patient safety across a
batch, a **recall** decision and regulatory notification may follow. Recall
classes (I/II/III) reflect the severity of the hazard.

## Trending

Individual complaints rarely tell the whole story — complaint **trends** by
product, defect type, and batch feed the Product Quality Review and CAPA system.
