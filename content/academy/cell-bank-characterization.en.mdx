# Cell Bank Establishment & Characterization

Every batch of a biologic ultimately traces back to a vial of cells. To keep the
product consistent over a product's entire life, manufacturers build a **two-tiered
cell bank system** and characterize it thoroughly — it's the genetic foundation of
"the process defines the product."

## The two-tiered system: MCB and WCB

- **Master Cell Bank (MCB)** — the definitive, fully characterized source,
  created once from a single clone. Finite and precious; accessed rarely.
- **Working Cell Bank (WCB)** — expanded from one MCB vial and used for routine
  production. When a WCB runs low, you make a new WCB from the MCB — not a new MCB.

This protects the original source, limits handling of the MCB, and ensures every
batch descends from the same characterized origin.

## What characterization covers

A cell bank is qualified across several dimensions (ICH Q5A/Q5B/Q5D):

- **Identity** — the cells are what they should be (isoenzyme/STR, species).
- **Purity / sterility** — free of bacteria, fungi, mycoplasma.
- **Viral safety** — adventitious and endogenous virus testing (pillar 1 of viral
  safety).
- **Genetic stability** — the construct/expression is stable across the
  generations used in manufacturing (gene copy, sequence).
- **Productivity / phenotype** — consistent expression of the right product.

## Generational control (limit of in vitro cell age)

You qualify cells up to a defined **limit of in vitro cell age** — the maximum
number of population doublings/passages from MCB through end of production.
Manufacturing stays within that validated window, so cells are never used "older"
than what was shown to be stable.

## Why it matters

The cell bank is the single most upstream control in biologics. A weak cell-bank
foundation undermines everything downstream — which is why characterization is
extensive and why comparability after any cell-line change is a major exercise.

> Tip: when tracing a quality trend, don't stop at the process — confirm which WCB
> the affected batches came from. Cell-bank generation is an upstream variable
> that's easy to overlook and occasionally the real root cause.
