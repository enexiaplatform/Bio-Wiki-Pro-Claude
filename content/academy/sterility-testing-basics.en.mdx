# Sterility Testing — Foundations

The sterility test confirms a product is free of viable, growth-capable
micro-organisms, per **USP &lt;71&gt;** and **EP 2.6.1**.

## Two compendial methods

- **Membrane filtration** — preferred for filterable solutions.
- **Direct inoculation** — when the product cannot be filtered.

## Media & incubation

| Medium | Target | Temperature |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FTM | Anaerobic & aerobic bacteria | 30–35°C |
| SCDM / TSB | Fungi & aerobic bacteria | 20–25°C |

Minimum incubation time is **14 days**.

> Note: always run method suitability (bacteriostasis/fungistasis) before
> using results for a commercial batch.
