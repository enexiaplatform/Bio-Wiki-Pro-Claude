# Data Integrity — Audit Trail Review (Deep Dive)

ALCOA+ defines the principles; the hard part is operationalising **audit trail
review** so data integrity is actually assured day to day.

## Risk-based review

Not every audit trail needs the same scrutiny. Tier systems by impact:
chromatography integration changes and result reprocessing are high-risk and
warrant routine review; low-risk metadata can be reviewed by exception.

## What reviewers look for

- Reprocessed or re-integrated results
- Aborted/incomplete runs and "test" injections
- Changes to methods, sequences, or clocks
- Disabled audit trails or shared logins

## Who and how often

Review by someone independent of the analyst, at a defined frequency tied to
risk (e.g. per batch for high-risk systems). Document the review — "we review
audit trails" without records is a finding.
