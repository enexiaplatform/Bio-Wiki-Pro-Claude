# Stability Studies (ICH Q1A)

Stability testing establishes how a product's quality changes over time and sets
the **shelf life** and **storage conditions** on the label.

## Standard conditions (Zone II)

| Study | Condition | Min duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Long-term | 25°C / 60% RH | 12 months (at submission) |
| Intermediate | 30°C / 65% RH | 6 months |
| Accelerated | 40°C / 75% RH | 6 months |

## What's tested

CQAs that may change over time — assay, degradation products, dissolution,
moisture, microbial quality, container-closure integrity.

## Bracketing & matrixing

For multiple strengths/pack sizes, ICH Q1D allows reduced designs (bracketing,
matrixing) when scientifically justified — saving significant testing.
