# Aseptic Technique — Fundamentals

In aseptic processing the operator is the biggest contamination risk. Technique —
not equipment alone — keeps the product sterile.

## First air

"First air" is HEPA-filtered air before it touches any surface. Critical
operations must be performed in first air, and nothing (hands, tools) may pass
between the filter and the open product.

## Core behaviours

- **Slow, deliberate movements** to avoid disrupting unidirectional airflow.
- **Never reach across** open containers or break first air.
- **Correct gowning** and routine gown qualification.
- **Minimise interventions** — every intervention is a contamination opportunity.

## Why it matters

Media fills (aseptic process simulations) validate the line *and* the operators.
A single poor technique habit can fail a media fill and halt production.
