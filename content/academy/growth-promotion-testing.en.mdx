# Growth Promotion Testing (GPT)

Growth Promotion Testing proves that a batch of culture media can actually
support microbial growth. Without it, a "no growth" sterility or bioburden
result is meaningless — you can't tell sterile from dead media.

## What you test

- **Challenge organisms** — compendial strains (e.g. *S. aureus*, *P. aeruginosa*,
  *B. subtilis*, *C. albicans*, *A. brasiliensis*) plus relevant environmental isolates.
- **Low inoculum** — typically **≤ 100 CFU** to challenge sensitivity.

## Acceptance criteria

- **Solid media:** recovery within a factor of 2 of the reference lot.
- **Liquid media:** clearly visible turbidity/growth within the specified time.

## Common pitfalls

- Using too high an inoculum (masks a weak medium).
- Not testing each lot, including in-house prepared media.
- Ignoring expiry and storage conditions that degrade media performance.
