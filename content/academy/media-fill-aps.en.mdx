# Media Fill & Aseptic Process Simulation

An Aseptic Process Simulation (APS), or media fill, substitutes sterile growth
medium for product to demonstrate that the aseptic process can reliably produce
a sterile product. EU GMP Annex 1 (2022) treats it as a cornerstone of the
Contamination Control Strategy.

## Acceptance criteria (Annex 1)

- Fewer than 5,000 units: **no contaminated units** should be detected.
- 5,000 to 10,000 units: one contaminated unit triggers investigation and a
  consideration of a repeat; two triggers revalidation after investigation.
- More than 10,000 units: one contaminated unit triggers investigation; two
  trigger revalidation.

## Designing for worst case

A credible APS reproduces the worst-case conditions the process actually faces:
maximum interventions, shift changes, maximum number of operators, the longest
permitted run time, and the most challenging container/closure.
