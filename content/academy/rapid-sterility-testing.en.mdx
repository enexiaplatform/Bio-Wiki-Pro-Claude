# Rapid Sterility Testing

The compendial sterility test (**USP &lt;71&gt;**) takes **14 days** — a problem
for short-shelf-life products. Rapid sterility methods cut that dramatically.

## The technologies

- **Growth-based** rapid detection (faster signal of growth)
- **Viability-based** (e.g. ATP bioluminescence, solid-phase cytometry)
- **Nucleic-acid-based** methods

## Where rapid sterility wins

Cell and gene therapies, radiopharmaceuticals, and compounded sterile products
may expire before a 14-day test finishes — rapid methods enable timely release.

## Validation is the gate

Per **USP &lt;1223&gt;** / Ph. Eur. 5.1.6, demonstrate the rapid method is
equivalent (or superior) to the compendial test for your matrix: detection
limit, specificity, robustness, and a documented equivalence package. The method
isn't the audit risk — the validation file is.
