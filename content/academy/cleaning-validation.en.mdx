# Cleaning Validation

Cleaning validation documents that a cleaning procedure consistently reduces
residues (product, degradants, cleaning agents, bioburden) below safe limits —
preventing cross-contamination in shared equipment.

## Acceptance limits

Modern limits are based on **Health-Based Exposure Limits (HBEL / PDE)** per the
EMA guideline, rather than the older 1/1000-dose and 10-ppm criteria alone.

## Sampling methods

- **Swab sampling** — direct, for hard-to-reach "worst-case" locations.
- **Rinse sampling** — covers large surface areas indirectly.

## Worst-case logic

Validate the hardest-to-clean product, equipment train, and locations. A
successful worst case justifies a grouping/bracketing approach.
