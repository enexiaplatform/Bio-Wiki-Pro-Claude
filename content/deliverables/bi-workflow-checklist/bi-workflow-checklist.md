# Biological Indicator (BI) Workflow Checklist

General educational workflow — verify against the applicable regulations and
standards, your approved SOPs, and your validated quality system. Not a
substitute for QA approval or a validated method.

---

## 1. BI selection

- [ ] Organism matches the process (G. stearothermophilus for steam/VHP; B. atrophaeus for dry heat/EO)
- [ ] Certified population and D-value on the CoA suit the process
- [ ] BI within expiry and stored per the manufacturer's instructions

## 2. Placement

- [ ] BIs placed at validated worst-case (hardest-to-sterilize) locations from the load/heat-penetration study
- [ ] Placement map documented for the load configuration

## 3. Exposure and recovery

- [ ] Cycle run and physical parameters recorded (temperature, time, F0)
- [ ] BIs transferred to recovery media within the labeled hold time (avoid false negatives)
- [ ] Positive and negative controls included for every run

## 4. Incubation and reading

- [ ] Incubated at the specified temperature and time
- [ ] Read for growth (turbidity or indicator change)
- [ ] Results recorded against the controls

## 5. Interpretation

- [ ] All exposed BIs negative AND controls valid supports the kill claim
- [ ] Positive control shows growth (proves BI viability and media suitability)
- [ ] Any unexpected positive treated as an excursion until investigated

## 6. Investigation and release

- [ ] Unexpected positive: verify controls and aseptic recovery first
- [ ] Review cycle physical data before drawing any conclusion
- [ ] Investigation completed and documented before releasing the cycle/load
