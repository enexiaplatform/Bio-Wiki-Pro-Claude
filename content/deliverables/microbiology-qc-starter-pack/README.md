# Microbiology QC Starter Pack — Start Here

An orientation pack for new QC microbiology analysts and lab supervisors: the core
daily tests, what good looks like, the early mistakes to avoid, and a first-90-days
plan to get qualified.

## What's inside

1. **Microbiology QC Starter Guide (PDF)** — the core QC micro tests, key controls
   ("what good looks like"), common early mistakes, and where to go deeper.
2. **First 90 Days Checklist (CSV/Excel)** — a week-by-week onboarding plan.

## How to use it

1. Read the starter guide first to see the whole landscape.
2. Work the first-90-days checklist with your supervisor.
3. Go deeper with the **Microbiology QC** workflows (Culture Media Selection,
   Environmental Monitoring, Biological Indicator) and the **Microbiology QC
   Fundamentals** learning path.

## Important

General educational workflow. Verify against the applicable pharmacopeia
(USP/EP/JP), your approved SOPs, and your validated quality system. Not a substitute
for QA approval, formal training, or a validated method.
