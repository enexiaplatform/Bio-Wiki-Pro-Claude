# Environmental Monitoring Checklist — Start Here

A practical, audit-ready checklist for designing and running a risk-based
environmental monitoring (EM) program for classified areas — aligned with the
contamination control strategy (CCS) thinking in Annex 1 (2022).

## What's inside

1. **EM Program Checklist (PDF)** — seven sections covering program design,
   methods & frequency, media & incubation, sampling execution, data & trending,
   excursion response, and audit readiness. Work through each box.
2. **EM Site & Sample Plan (CSV/Excel)** — a template to record each sample
   location, grade, method, frequency, and alert/action limits, with columns for
   the latest result and trend.

## How to use it

1. Run the checklist when setting up or reviewing an EM program, or before an
   inspection.
2. Use the site & sample plan to document your locations and limits — auditors
   expect site selection to be justified by risk, not convenience.
3. Pair this with the **Environmental Monitoring** workflow for the step-by-step
   method.

## Important

General educational workflow. Verify against the applicable regulations
(e.g. Annex 1, USP), your approved SOPs, and your validated quality system. Not a
substitute for QA approval or a validated method.
