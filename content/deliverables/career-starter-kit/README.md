# Career Starter Kit

Everything to package yourself well for a QC/QA role in Pharma, Biotech, or Life
Science. Use in this order:

1. **QC/QA CV Template** (`qc-qa-cv-template.md`) — a results-focused structure
   built for QC/QA hiring managers, with worked example bullet points.
2. **Cover Letter Templates** (`cover-letter-templates.md`) — three versions
   (entry, senior, career-changer) you can adapt in minutes.
3. **LinkedIn Profile Guide** (`linkedin-profile-guide.md`) — headline, About, and
   skills that recruiters search for in this field.
4. **Top Pharma/Biotech Employers** (`top-pharma-employers.csv`) — a starter target
   list to research, open in Excel/Sheets and add your own.

These are templates and guidance, not a guarantee of any outcome. Make every claim
on your CV true and verifiable.
