## The 8 rules QC/QA hiring managers care about
1. **Quantify.** "Improved" is invisible; "reduced OOS recurrence 40%" is hired.
2. **Show the system, not the task.** Not "did CAPAs" — "drove CAPA to verified
   effectiveness."
3. **Name the standards** you've worked to (Annex 1, ICH, Part 11) — it signals
   real exposure.
4. **Mirror the advert's keywords** (many CVs are filtered by ATS first).
5. **Lead with outcomes**, list duties only if they add signal.
6. **One page if <8 years**, two if more. Never three.
7. **No typos.** In QA, a sloppy CV is a data-integrity red flag.
8. **Every claim true and defensible** — you will be asked about each line.
