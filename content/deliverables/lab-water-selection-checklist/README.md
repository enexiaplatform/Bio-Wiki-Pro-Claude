# Lab Water Selection Checklist — Start Here

A short, practical checklist to choose and justify the right water grade for a
test or process step — and to define the specs and monitoring that go with it.

## What's inside

1. **Lab Water Selection Checklist (PDF)** — five sections: define the use, select
   the grade, specifications & monitoring, system & controls, and documentation.
2. **Water Grade Quick Reference (CSV/Excel)** — typical use, conductivity/TOC,
   microbial limit, and endotoxin for each grade.

## How to use it

1. Work the checklist when choosing water for a new method or step, or when an
   auditor asks how you justify the grade.
2. Use the quick reference to sanity-check specs against the grade.
3. Pair this with the **Lab Water Type Selector** tool for a quick recommendation.

## Important

General educational workflow. Verify against the applicable pharmacopeia
(USP/EP/JP), your approved SOPs, and your validated water system. Not a substitute
for QA approval or a validated method.
