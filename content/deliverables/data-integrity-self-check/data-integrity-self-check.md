# Data Integrity Self-Check (ALCOA+)

General educational workflow — verify against the applicable regulations and
data-integrity guidance, your approved SOPs, and your validated systems. Not a
substitute for QA approval or a validated method.

---

## 1. System inventory and data lifecycle

- [ ] All GMP data systems inventoried (paper, hybrid, electronic)
- [ ] Data lifecycle mapped: generate, process, review, report, retain, dispose
- [ ] Criticality of each system assessed

## 2. ALCOA+ per record type

- [ ] Attributable — who recorded it and when; unique logins
- [ ] Legible and permanent — no pencil, no overwriting
- [ ] Contemporaneous — recorded at the time of the activity
- [ ] Original — the first record, or a verified true copy
- [ ] Accurate — correct, with errors corrected transparently
- [ ] Complete, Consistent, Enduring, and Available

## 3. Audit trails and access

- [ ] Audit trails enabled and cannot be disabled by users
- [ ] Audit-trail / exception review is part of routine data review
- [ ] Unique logins, role-based access, and segregation of duties
- [ ] No shared or admin accounts used for routine work

## 4. Spreadsheets and standalone systems

- [ ] GMP spreadsheets validated, formulas locked, and version-controlled
- [ ] Standalone instruments have controlled, attributable data handling
- [ ] Backups and retention defined and tested

## 5. Gaps and remediation

- [ ] Gaps documented and ranked by risk
- [ ] CAPA driven on the highest-risk gaps first
- [ ] Periodic data-integrity monitoring in place
