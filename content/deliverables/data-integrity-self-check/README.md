# Data Integrity Self-Check — Start Here

A structured self-assessment to check your records against ALCOA+ and find the
gaps before an inspector does — across paper, hybrid, and electronic systems.

## What's inside

1. **Data Integrity Self-Check (PDF)** — five sections: system inventory & data
   lifecycle, ALCOA+ per record type, audit trails & access, spreadsheets &
   standalone systems, and gaps & remediation.
2. **ALCOA+ Gap Log (CSV/Excel)** — a template to record each finding by system
   and principle, with risk, action, owner, and status.

## How to use it

1. Work the self-check per system, focusing on the highest-risk data.
2. Log every gap and rank it by risk; drive CAPA on the worst first.
3. Pair this with the **Data Integrity Review** workflow for the method.

## Important

General educational workflow. Verify against the applicable regulations and
data-integrity guidance, your approved SOPs, and your validated systems. Not a
substitute for QA approval or a validated method.
