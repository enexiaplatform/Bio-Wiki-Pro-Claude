# Interview Prep Pack (QC/QA)

100+ real-style QC/QA interview questions, grouped by theme, plus a framework for
answering the ones that decide the offer.

- **`qc-qa-interview-questions.md`** — the full question bank with model-answer
  guidance for the highest-stakes questions.

How to use it: don't memorize answers. Pick the 20 questions most relevant to the
role, prepare a real example for each (using the STAR structure inside), and
rehearse out loud with a colleague playing the interviewer.
